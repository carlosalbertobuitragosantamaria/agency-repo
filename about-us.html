About Us

The creators of our Space Pets, the visionary minds behind this extraordinary venture, are a dynamic duo driven by their shared passion for interplanetary exploration and the love for unique pets

Meet Shaughn, an astrophysicist with a deep understanding of celestial bodies and a boundless curiosity about the mysteries of the universe. With a background in space research and a profound appreciation for the wonders beyond Earth, Shaughn brings scientific expertise and a keen eye for identifying the potential of these otherworldly pets.

Carlos is seasoned animal enthusiast and a compassionate advocate for responsible pet ownership. Drawing from their extensive experience in animal care and welfare, Carlos ensures that the well-being and ethical considerations of these unique companions are at the forefront of the agency's mission.
